The marvels of space in your field of vision

Space and its satellites are beautiful. Our goal is to bring the awe from the the satellites orbiting the Earth to you. By using NASA and N2Yo's APIs, we can give you a list of all the satellites visible in you field of vision depending on your location and the current day. Click on a satellite for more information.
